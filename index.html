What It Does
  • Persistent memory with Ebbinghaus-style decay and retrieval reinforcement
  • 7-level trust progression — personality depth unlocks through real conversation

  • Somatic marker system — gut feeling generated before each response

  • Response preference tracking — learns what resonates over time
