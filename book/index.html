The Book What This Is
"Most LLM books teach you to call an API. This one teaches you what happens after — memory, trust, emotion, safety, and why it matters for the organizations deploying these systems."

This book grew out of building Unleashed AI — a production LLM system with persistent memory, multi-phase inner thought pipelines, trust-gated disclosure, real-time emotion modeling, crisis detection, and relationship health monitoring. Every chapter is grounded in that real implementation, cross-referenced against the research literature that informed it.

The target reader is a technical decision-maker: engineers who will build the system, architects who will design it, and government or enterprise leaders who need to understand what they are commissioning and why each design choice has consequences for their users and their organization.

The book covers LLM fundamentals through to advanced deployment concerns — alignment, hallucination, memory architecture, emotion-aware prompting, safety systems, governance frameworks, and what responsible AI adoption actually looks like inside an organization or agency.
